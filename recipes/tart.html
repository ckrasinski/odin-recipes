Tart

Description

This incredibly easy, no-bake chocolate tart is made with a simple Oreo crust and rich chocolate ganache filling made with both milk and dark chocolates. You won’t be able to eat just one piece!

Ingredients

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 6 tablespoons (85g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup (170g, 6 oz.) coarsely chopped bittersweet chocolate
  • 1 cup (170g, 6 oz.) coarsely chopped milk chocolate
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • ¼ cup (56g) unsalted butter, cut into small pieces

Steps

  1. Pulse Oreo cookies, with filling, in a food processor until finely ground.
  2. Transfer crumbs to a medium bowl, add melted butter, and mix until combined and moistened.
  3. Press the mixture onto the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch (23cm) tart pan.
  4. Place it in the fridge for 30 minutes to firm up before adding the filling (or before baking the crust).
  5. In a medium heatproof bowl, place chopped bittersweet and milk chocolate.
  6. In a small saucepan over medium heat, bring the heavy cream and butter to a low boil.
  7. Remove from the heat and pour mixture over the chocolate.
  8. Let stand for 1 minute.
  9. Using a rubber spatula, stir mixture until melted and smooth (if not completely melted, heat in the microwave for several seconds and stir, until melted).
  10. Pour mixture over chilled Oreo crust and refrigerate until set, at least 4 hours or overnight.
